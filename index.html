THE TAJ MAHAL: ONE OF THE 7 WONDERS OF THE WORLD

by Taj Dawkins

the taj mahal is one of the 7 wonders wonders of the world, it was commissioned in 1631 by the fifth Mughal emperor, Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. The structure covers a giant 42 acres of land!
